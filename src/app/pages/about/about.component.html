About Us

We are a passionate group dedicated to the preservation and protection of trees. Through education, community engagement, and advocacy, we work to raise awareness about the vital role trees play in our environment, health, and future. Join us in fostering a greener, more sustainable world, one tree at a time.

IIn today’s world, waste disposal is becoming a bigger issue, impacting both our environment and our health. Barangay Balibago, Angeles City, Pampanga, is no exception. With more waste being produced every day and limited ways to manage it properly, the community needs a smarter, more efficient system to handle waste responsibly. That’s where EcoGuard comes in—a digital platform designed to make waste disposal easier, encourage recycling, and promote environmental responsibility among residents.

That’s where EcoGuard acts as a bridge between the community and waste collection services, giving residents an easy-to-use platform to schedule pickups, learn about proper waste segregation, and track their environmental impact. By combining modern technology with existing waste management efforts, EcoGuard makes the process more accessible, efficient, and convenient. With real-time updates, educational resources, and interactive features, residents stay informed and engaged in keeping their surroundings clean.

At its core, EcoGuard is about building a greener future for Barangay Balibago. By working together—residents, local authorities, and environmental advocates—we can reduce waste, recycle more, and adopt responsible disposal habits. Our goal is to make Barangay Balibago a model for sustainable waste management, showing how a community-driven effort can lead to a cleaner, healthier, and more environmentally conscious future. Together, we can turn responsible waste management into a way of life!

Our Mission

To empower the residents of Barangay Balibago, Angeles City, Pampanga, through a digital waste management platform that fosters responsible disposal, recycling, and community engagement. By leveraging technology, we aim to educate citizens, streamline waste collection, and promote sustainable practices for a cleaner and greener environment.

Our Vision

To create a future where every household actively participates in responsible waste management, making Barangay Balibago a model of sustainability. Through innovation, education, and collaboration, EcoGuard envisions a cleaner, healthier, and environmentally conscious community for generations to come.
